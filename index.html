Glaiza Mae Tampus was born in Cebu City, Philippines, in 1996. She grew up in the small town of Talamban, where her parents were married. She holds a Bachelor of Science in information technology from the University of Cebu.

Glaiza is a software test engineer with more than 5-years of expertise in software testing. Her areas of expertise as a tester include analyzing and evaluating software design. She has worked as a software tester for Kyocera and Ernst and Young.

She generated test designs and scripts for the web and interactive media design for the quality assurance department, which is when she rediscovered her passion for front-end programming and web design. She became increasingly interested in designing and developing websites along the process, and she believes that education will help her grow her talents and that formal education in multimedia will truly benefit her.

Glaiza has now relocated to Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada, to pursue a diploma in Multimedia Production at Lethbridge College.

She enjoys photography and travels to discover new perspectives, learn more about culture, and freeze the moment when she is not working. She enjoys spending time with her daughter via video call since she is away from home.
